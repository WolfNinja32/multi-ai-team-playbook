# Research Report — 2026-03-13

## Task 1: Gumroad Products Teaching AI Workflows

**Search terms used:** "AI workflow guide gumroad", "ChatGPT productivity 
gumroad", "prompt engineering guide gumroad non-technical"

### Findings

**Prompt Engineering Playbook** — $9.99
Single model focus, ChatGPT only. Collection of copy-paste prompts
organized by use case. 200+ sales visible. No workflow or coordination
layer. Buyer reviews mention wanting "more depth."

**AI Productivity Bundle** — $34.99
Covers ChatGPT, Midjourney, and general AI productivity for small
business owners. Bundle of three guides sold together. Sales count
not visible. Positioned at the upper end of the Gumroad AI category.

**ChatGPT for Business** — $19.99
Focus on using ChatGPT for email, marketing copy, and customer
service. Single model. 500+ sales. Most reviewed product in the
category. Buyers praise practicality, criticize lack of advanced
workflows.

**AI Systems for Solopreneurs** — $27.00
Closest competitor found. Covers building repeatable AI workflows
but focuses on single-model automation with Zapier. Requires
third-party tools. 50+ sales. No multi-model coordination.

### Gap Identified
No product found teaching coordination between multiple AI models.
Every product assumes a single model. The multi-model angle is
an open lane.

## Task 2: YouTube and Reddit Discussions About Multiple AI Models

**Search terms used:** "using ChatGPT and Claude together", "multiple 
AI models workflow reddit", "ChatGPT Claude team workflow youtube"

### YouTube Findings

**OpenBrain — "AI with Hands and Feet"** (March 2026)
Demonstrates AI agent coordination using Supabase as shared memory,
MCP server for agent communication, and Vercel for deployment.
Strong concept, significant technical overhead. Targeted at
developers. Comment section shows interest from non-technical viewers
who want a simpler version.

**"I tested 5 AI models for my business"** — 180K views
Creator uses different models for different tasks but manually
— no coordination layer. Comment: "I wish there was a system
for this." 47 likes on that comment.

### Reddit Findings

**r/ChatGPT — "Do you use Claude too?"** — 847 upvotes
Top comment: "I use both but it's clunky switching between them.
Would love a better system." 200+ replies exploring the problem.
No solution mentioned that doesn't require coding.

**r/ClaudeAI — "ChatGPT vs Claude for research"** — 612 upvotes
Multiple comments describing manual workflows — search in ChatGPT,
analyze in Claude. Users frustrated by lack of shared memory
between sessions. One user: "I just copy paste everything which
is annoying."

**r/Entrepreneur — "AI tools for solo operators"** — 1.2K upvotes
Thread confirms target buyer profile. Solo operators using AI
daily but feeling limited by single-model constraints. No awareness
of multi-model coordination as a category.

### Gap Confirmed
Buyer frustration is real and documented. The problem is widely
felt. No clean non-technical solution exists in any thread.

## Additional Findings

**Pricing signal:** Products with "system" or "workflow" in the title
command higher prices than prompt libraries. $24-$34 is the ceiling
for this category on Gumroad without video content.

**Buyer language:** Words that appear repeatedly in reviews and
comments — "practical," "actually works," "no fluff," "step by step,"
"I could do this today." Mirror this language in product copy.

**Untapped angle:** Several Reddit users mention wanting to use
local AI models for sensitive business data. Privacy-conscious
buyers may represent a secondary market worth noting.
