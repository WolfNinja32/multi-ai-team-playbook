### AI with Hands and Feet — OpenBrain
**URL:** https://youtube.com [video reference]
**Platform:** YouTube / not a product for sale
**Price:** Free
**First seen:** 2026-03-12
**Last updated:** 2026-03-12
**Status:** Evaluated

**What it is:**
A YouTube video demonstrating an AI agent coordination stack
built on Supabase, an MCP server, and Vercel. Agents share
a single database as persistent memory.

**Relevance:**
Direct competitor concept. Their core insight matches ours —
AI agents need an external shared surface. Their implementation
requires three services and real configuration.

**Notes:**
Key differentiator identified: their stack requires Supabase,
MCP, and Vercel. Ours requires a free GitHub account. For a
solo operator with no coding background, that gap is the whole
ballgame. Used as primary competitive positioning in methodology doc.
