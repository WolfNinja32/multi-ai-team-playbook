## Success Criteria

A good result:
- Identifies real demand from actual buyers, not theoretical interest
- Flags competitive products with honest assessment of their strengths
- Surfaces pricing signals from comparable products on Gumroad
- Gives enough clarity to make a confident go/no-go decision

A poor result:
- Generic market overviews with no actionable specifics
- Optimistic framing that glosses over competitive threats
- Recommendations that require coding or technical setup

## My Constraints

- No coding required in any recommendation or workflow
- No tools requiring API keys or paid developer accounts
- Budget conscious — free tiers preferred where possible
- Products must be launchable by a solo operator without a team
- Time to market matters — ship simple and fast over ship complete
